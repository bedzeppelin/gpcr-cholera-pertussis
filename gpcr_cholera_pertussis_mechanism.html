Gs pathway · normal activation

AC ACTIVATED
Mechanism
Agonist binds the 7-TM GPCR. The receptor changes conformation, acting as a GEF: it catalyzes GDP→GTP exchange on Gα.
G protein dissociation
Gαs-GTP dissociates from Gβγ and from the receptor, then diffuses to bind and stimulate adenylyl cyclase.
cAMP output
AC catalyzes ATP → cAMP. Elevated cAMP activates PKA, driving downstream phosphorylation events.
